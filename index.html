Shalakh Sam Varughese

Composer & Sound Designer for Visual Media

I am Shalakh Sam Varughese, a composer based in Boston, MA. My passion for film music began early, shaped by iconic films like Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone. As a composer, my goal is to create music that elevates a scene without overshadowing the story.

Currently, I am completing my Bachelor's degree in Film Scoring at Berklee College of Music. Alongside my studies, I work with composer Akram Haddad (orchestrator for Assassin's Creed Mirage), assisting with arranging, orchestrating, and preparing professional scores. I also serve as Supervisor and Technology Assistant for the Ensemble Department, managing auditions and contributing to the development of the department's flagship app.

In addition to my film scoring studies, I am expanding into game scoring and sound design. I primarily play piano but my expertise expands to other instruments like organ, guitar, ukulele, drums, congas and basic violin. This versatility brings a strong understanding of how different instruments function in an orchestra.

I am looking to collaborate with filmmakers and developers who value music as an integral, intentional part in the narrative experience.
